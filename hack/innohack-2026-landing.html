Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about InnoHack 2026. Can't find your answer? Contact us!

Who can participate in InnoHack 2026?
+
Any college student from any discipline can participate! Whether you're studying CS, design, business, or any other field‚Äîif you're passionate about innovation, you're welcome. Teams can have 1-4 members. No age limit, just bring your student ID.
I'm a complete beginner. Can I still participate?
+
Absolutely! We welcome hackers of all skill levels. We'll have beginner-friendly workshops, dedicated mentors for first-timers, starter kits, and resources to help you learn. Many past winners were first-time hackers. Everyone starts somewhere‚ÄîInnoHack is the perfect place!
What should I bring to the event?
+
Bring your laptop, chargers, valid student ID, any hardware you want to use, comfortable clothes, and your creativity! We'll provide all meals, unlimited drinks, sleeping bags, WiFi, workstations, and swag. Just bring yourself and your ideas.
Can I work on an existing project?
+
No, all projects must be built from scratch during the 36-hour hackathon. However, you can use existing libraries, APIs, frameworks, and open-source tools. The core idea and implementation must be new. We check timestamps and have strict plagiarism detection.
What's included in the ‚Çπ400 registration fee?
+
Everything! You get: 36-hour venue access, all meals & unlimited caffeine, official InnoHack swag kit & T-shirt, certificate of participation, access to all workshops & mentorship, networking opportunities, eligibility for ‚Çπ5L+ prizes, and post-event community access.
Is there a refund policy?
+
Full refunds available until Feb 10, 2026. After Feb 10, registration fees are non-refundable as we finalize catering, swag, and venue. However, you can transfer your registration to another team member or friend until Feb 20.
How do I find teammates?
+
Join our Discord, WhatsApp, or Telegram communities (links in footer) to connect with other participants! We'll also have an official team formation session during the opening ceremony. Come solo, leave with lifelong friends and co-founders!
Are there travel reimbursements?
+
Limited travel reimbursements available for participants from outside Hyderabad. Priority given to top talent from far locations. Details shared with registered teams on a case-by-case basis. We also help arrange group transportation from major cities.
